Custom PC material

Exclusively for CYBERBOARD Terminal, we custom-made a semi-transparent black PC material that doesn't require a coating of paint. After mixing the colors, the base material is directly sandblasted to achieve a delicate semi-transparent effect. This improved production process is not only more eco-friendly, but is also extremely durable and incredibly beautiful.

Digital Rain dynamic lighting effect

Digital Rain, which symbolizes Terminal’s command-line, flies across the LED panel like it's on an endless search for information. With a special after-effect from each keystroke, it’s as geeky as it gets.

Advanced translucent Gasket Mount with integrated design

As our team is continuously experimenting to further improve the touch and feel of CYBERBOARD, we came up with a redesigned gasket structure. Implementing the Gasket Mount into a PC keyboard goes further than just ensuring a good typing experience. The materials and appearance on the inside should also be up to par.

Differing from the previous iteration of Gasket Mount, we used silicone with a hardness level of 10. The silicone is integrated with the plate through injection molding, which not only looks more transparent by blending in with the keyboard's overall design, but also sounds less hollow and gives it a more solid feel.

The integrated plate and silicone pads provide a more consistent internal structure. Gaps are less pronounced than in conventional structures.

Mirror PVD stainless steel weight

Unlike metal, our semi-transparent PC material provides a unique lighting experience. However, to achieve the same substantial feel as when using metal, a counterweight is required.

We created a new radical design for the counterweight of CYBERBOARD Terminal, with the weight plate being visible from the exterior. Its mirror PVD stainless steel surface occupies most of the bottom area. Exposed reflective stainless steel forms a stark, yet dazzling contrast with the matte PC material.

Choose between engraved and non-engraved keycaps

The switches are another fundamental aspect of CYBERBOARD Terminal's unique retro look. We chose Glacier Black keycaps and EQUALZ Kiwi switches and stabilizers. Kiwi switches, with their unique translucent bright green color, add a touch of mystery to the Glacier Black keycaps - perfectly complimenting Terminal's classic black and green color scheme.

As for the keycaps, both engraved and non-engraved are available. Non-engraved keycaps are a great choice for those who're looking for a bolder and more aggressive look.

*During testing, we liked the feel of Icy Silver switches when used for the space bar. Therefore, we decided to include an additional Icy Silver switch in the Terminal Bundle.

Exclusive keyboard case

Our keyboard case is pretty hardcore, but the black and white color scheme is not really consistent with the retro black and green colors of CYBERBOARD Terminal. That’s why we designed the case with a black and green color scheme, achieving that full hacker look.
